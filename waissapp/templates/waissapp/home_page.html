WAISS ETCal

WAISS ETCal is an ET-based Irrigation Scheduler and Calculator which analyzes evapotranspiration rates and translates it into manageable and simple advisories for management of crops. Evapotranspiration rates can be determined directly through specialized evapotranspiration instruments or can be computed from agrometeorological data such as temperature, humidity, sunshine duration, etc. through empirical formulas.

{% endblock %}

Who can use it?


This web application is for everyone's use!

Farm People

including farmers, farm managers, operators, owners, extension workers and anyone who works in a farm

Technical Persons

including engineers, technicians, data processing workers, researchers, teachers, students, etc.

Others

all interested users with or without on-site data sources such as atmometers, AWS, ET controllers, etc.

Why use SARAi technologies?



This web application uses evapotranspiration or agrometeorological data to generate an irrigation schedule and irrigation advisories for farms.

This enables accurate crop water and irrigation water management especially for farmers and farm workers, continuous and distanced monitoring for agricultural extension workers and managers, and a handy irrigation scheduler and calculator for students, teachers, researchers, etc.
