With Water Advisory for Irrigation Scheduling System or WAISS, farmers can apply the right amount of water to crops at the right time and in the right place.

It is project of the PCAARRD-funded SARAI Project 2.3: Smarter Technologies for Crop Water Management under Project Leader Dr. Roger A Luyun Jr. and Study Leader Dr. Ronaldo B. Saludes.

The team is composed by Agricultural and Biosystems Engineers Christian Martin Casedo, Ginalyn Robel Brazil, Jay Ann Lomod, Toni-An Mae Salcedo, Bryan Baltazar, and Jan Albert Atienza.

Read more on DOST-PCAARRD's page.

About Project SARAi

Project SARAi aims to develop a national crop forecasting and monitoring system for nine priority crops: rice, corn, banana, coconut, coffee, cacao, sugarcane, tomato, and soybean. This project is funded by DOST-PCAARRD.
