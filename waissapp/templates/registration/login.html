HOW CAN IT HELP YOU

WAISS generates real-time irrigation advisory based on actual soil moisture conditions, soil, crop, and irrigation parameters. Management Allowable Deficit (MAD) is a portion of the readily available amount of water stored in the soil that is allowed to deplete before irrigation. Whenever this is reached, the WAISS generates irrigation advisory to prevent the plants from experiencing water stress. The irrigation volume and irrigation time computations are specific to the irrigation system type (basin, border, furrow, drip, and sprinkler). WAISS supports farmers, agricultural extension workers, students, and researchers in implementing good irrigation practices by conserving water, reducing cost for irrigation, and preventing yield loss due to water stress.
